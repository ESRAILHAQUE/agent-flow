# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Project Name

**AgentFlow – AI Business Automation Platform**

---

# 1. Executive Summary

AgentFlow is an AI-powered business automation platform designed to help companies automate customer support, knowledge management, lead handling, email communication, and internal business operations.

The platform will combine Large Language Models (LLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), Tool Calling, Multi-Agent Systems, and Workflow Automation to create a centralized AI workspace for businesses.

---

# 2. Business Problem

Many businesses spend significant time and resources on:

* Responding to customer inquiries
* Managing leads
* Answering repetitive questions
* Writing emails
* Searching internal documents
* Generating reports

These processes are often manual, expensive, and inefficient.

The goal is to reduce operational costs, improve response time, and increase team productivity through AI automation.

---

# 3. Business Objectives

### Primary Objectives

* Automate customer support operations
* Enable AI-powered document search
* Automate email communication
* Improve lead management workflows
* Reduce support workload
* Increase operational efficiency

### Success Criteria

* Reduce customer response time by 70%
* Reduce support costs by 50%
* Achieve AI response accuracy above 85%
* Maintain 99.9% system uptime

# 5. Functional Requirements

## FR-01 Authentication & Authorization

The system shall provide:

* User registration
* User login
* Password reset
* Email verification
* Google OAuth login
* Role-based access control

Roles:

* Super Admin
* Organization Owner
* Team Member

---

## FR-02 Workspace Management

Organization Owners shall be able to:

* Create workspaces
* Invite team members
* Assign roles
* Manage organization settings

---

## FR-03 AI Chat Assistant

Users shall be able to communicate with an AI assistant.

Example:

User:
"What is your refund policy?"

AI:
"According to your company policy, customers can request a refund within 30 days of purchase."

---

## FR-04 Knowledge Base Management

Users shall be able to upload:

* PDF files
* DOCX files
* TXT files

The system shall:

* Extract document content
* Split content into chunks
* Generate embeddings
* Store vectors in a vector database

---

## FR-05 Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

The AI assistant shall answer questions using uploaded company knowledge.

Workflow:

Question → Vector Search → Relevant Context → LLM → Response

The system shall:

* Retrieve relevant document chunks
* Include retrieved context in prompts
* Generate accurate responses

---

## FR-06 Tool Calling

The AI assistant shall be capable of invoking external tools.

### Weather Tool

Capabilities:

* Retrieve current weather information

---

### Email Tool

Capabilities:

* Send emails
* Draft emails
* Schedule email delivery

---

### CRM Tool

Capabilities:

* Create leads
* Update lead information
* Retrieve lead details

---

### Database Tool

Capabilities:

* Retrieve customer information
* Update records
* Search business data

---

## FR-07 Conversation Memory

The system shall maintain:

* Conversation history
* User preferences
* Session context
* Persistent memory

The AI should generate context-aware responses.

## FR-08 Multi-Agent Workflow

The platform shall support multiple specialized AI agents.

### Manager Agent

Responsibilities:

* Analyze requests

---

### Research Agent

Responsibilities:

* Gather information
* Retrieve knowledge

---

### Writer Agent

Responsibilities:

* Generate responses
* Draft content

---

### Reviewer Agent

Responsibilities:

* Verify response quality
* Check accuracy and formatting

## FR-09 Analytics Dashboard

The system shall provide:
